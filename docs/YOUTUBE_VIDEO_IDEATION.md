## 5-PART SCRIPT FORMULA

Every video script generated by Agent 2 (narrate-project.ts) MUST follow this sequence:

```yaml
script_formula:
  name: "Hook → Credibility Drop → Bridge → Roadmap → Analogy Transition"
  parts:

    1_HOOK:
      duration_seconds: 15–30
      rule: >
        Open with the audience's PROBLEM or DESIRE — not a greeting, not a title card.
        First 3 words must be about THEM, not about you.
      anti_pattern: "Hi everyone, today we're going to talk about..."
      good_pattern: "[Pain point] is costing seafood buyers [consequence]."
      agent_prompt_hint: "Start script at the moment of maximum tension or curiosity."

    2_CREDIBILITY_DROP:
      duration_seconds: 20–40
      rule: >
        One specific, verifiable credential, case, or data point that proves
        you have earned the right to teach this topic. No generic claims.
      anti_pattern: "I've been in the seafood industry for years..."
      good_pattern: "We certified [X] fisheries across [Y] countries using this exact framework."
      agent_prompt_hint: "Pull one real fact from source material before writing this section."

    3_BRIDGE:
      duration_seconds: 15–25
      rule: >
        Connect their problem (Hook) to the solution you're about to teach.
        This is the 'so here's what I found' moment.
      anti_pattern: Jumping straight from credential to teaching without connection.
      good_pattern: "So when I saw [problem], I went back to [credibility source] and found..."
      agent_prompt_hint: "Explicit transition sentence required. Do not skip."

    4_ROADMAP:
      duration_seconds: 20–30
      rule: >
        Tell them EXACTLY what they will learn in the next N minutes.
        Named steps increase watch time. '3 things' beats 'some things'.
      anti_pattern: "Stay until the end for the best part."
      good_pattern: "In the next 8 minutes I'll show you: [1], [2], and [3]."
      agent_prompt_hint: "Generate numbered list of video sections. Insert as roadmap."

    5_ANALOGY_TRANSITION:
      duration_seconds: 15–20
      rule: >
        One analogy that makes the technical topic accessible to a non-expert.
        Use the analogy again at the end of the video to close the loop.
      anti_pattern: Technical terminology without translation.
      good_pattern: "Think of seafood traceability like a passport — every fish needs one."
      agent_prompt_hint: >
        Choose analogy from: food safety / passport / supply chain / construction.
        Store the analogy in the script metadata so it can be echoed at conclusion.
```

## CONTENT DELIVERY RULES

```yaml
content_rules:

  STORY_WRAP:
    rule: "Every fact must be wrapped in a story, case, or consequence. No naked facts."
    test: "Can a 16-year-old understand why this fact matters? If no → add story."

  NO_NAKED_FACTS:
    rule: "Do not lead with statistics without attaching them to a person, place, or decision."
    anti_pattern: "40% of seafood is mislabeled in the US."
    good_pattern: "When a buyer in Seattle thought she was serving halibut, she was serving tilapia. That's part of the 40% mislabeling problem."

  CANCEL_CONDITION:
    rule: "If the script has facts without story in the first 60 seconds → STOP. Rewrite Hook first."
    agent_instruction: "narrate-project.ts must check: does first paragraph start with a human situation or a statistic?"

  FORMULA_ENFORCEMENT:
    rule: "All 5 parts of the script formula must be present. Missing any part → incomplete."
    agent_instruction: "At script completion, validate: hook=true, credibility_drop=true, bridge=true, roadmap=true, analogy=true."
```

## ANTI-PATTERNS (Do NOT Do These)

```yaml
anti_patterns:
  - "Putting revenue, money, or income in the title — attracts wrong audience"
  - "Opening with 'Welcome back to my channel' — loses 30% of viewers in 5 seconds"
  - "Listing facts without story — violates STORY_WRAP rule"
  - "Skipping the Credibility Drop — viewers don't know why to trust you"
  - "Vague roadmaps ('we'll cover a lot today') — watch time drops"
  - "Using the analogy only once — reuse it at the close to complete the loop"
  - "Producing without checking cancel_condition — wastes production budget"
```
