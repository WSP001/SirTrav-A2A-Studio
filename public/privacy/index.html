What we collect

When you authorize SirTrav via LinkedIn OAuth, we receive:

  • Your LinkedIn profile name and member ID (used to format posts)
  • An OAuth access token (stored securely in Netlify environment variables)

How we use it

Your LinkedIn credentials are used solely to publish video content on your behalf through the SirTrav A2A video automation pipeline. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data storage

Credentials are stored as encrypted environment variables in Netlify's infrastructure. They are never committed to source code or logged.

Your rights

You can revoke access at any time by removing the app from your LinkedIn Permitted Services settings.
